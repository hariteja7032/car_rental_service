Rental Information



Rental Period: Our rental period starts at 9:00 am and ends at 9:00 pm. We also offer hourly rental options, starting from a minimum of 4 hours.

Rental Rates: Our rental rates vary depending on the type of car and rental period. Please contact us for the latest rates and promotions.

Requirements: To rent a car from us, you must be at least 18 years old and possess a valid driving license. We may also require a copy of your identification card or passport.

Payment: Payment for rental services can be made in cash, by credit card or online payment methods.

Insurance: All our rental cars come with comprehensive insurance coverage, including third-party liability coverage. Additional insurance options may also be available upon request.

Fuel: Our rental cars are provided with a full tank of fuel. We expect the car to be returned with a full tank of fuel as well. If the car is not returned with a full tank of fuel, we will charge a refueling fee.

Maintenance: All our rental cars are well-maintained and undergo regular maintenance checks to ensure their safety and reliability.

Additional Services: We offer additional services such as GPS navigation systems, baby seats, and driver services at an additional cost. Please contact us for more information.

At our car rental service, we strive to provide the best possible rental experience for our customers. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
